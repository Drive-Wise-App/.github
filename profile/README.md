# Drive Wise

Drive Wise is an intelligent driver assistant application designed to help drivers make smarter decisions by analyzing ride offers and optimizing earnings.

---

## Features

- Smart ride filtering
- Earnings optimization
- Real-time alerts
- High-performance & lightweight
- Custom driver preferences

---

## Tech Stack

- Kotlin
- Android (Jetpack Compose)
- Clean Architecture
- MVVM
- Hilt (Dependency Injection)
- Retrofit
- Room Database
- TFLite ML kit
  
---

## 🔒 Data Privacy & Platform Compliance

Drive Wise is designed with a strong commitment to user privacy and platform compliance.

### 📊 Data Privacy
- Drive Wise does **not collect, store, or share any personal user data** without explicit consent.
- Any data processed (e.g., ride information) is handled **locally on the device** whenever possible.
- No information is transmitted to external servers.

### ⚖️ Platform Usage Disclaimer
- Drive Wise is an **independent tool** and is **not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Uber or any other ride-hailing service**.
- The app does **not access private APIs, reverse-engineer, or bypass security mechanisms** of any platform.
- Any displayed ride information is obtained through **user-visible content only** (e.g., accessibility-based reading), in compliance with platform policies.

### 🚫 Usage Responsibility
- Users are responsible for ensuring that their usage of Drive Wise complies with the terms of service of platforms such as Uber.
- The developer assumes **no liability** for misuse or violations of third-party platform policies.

### 🛡️ Ethical Use
- Drive Wise is built to **assist drivers by notifying them**, not to exploit or manipulate or simulate presses on the platform systems.
- The app focuses on improving decision-making while respecting fair usage and ecosystem integrity.
